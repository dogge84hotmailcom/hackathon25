Why I chose Chas and Javascript

I love helping poeple with anyhting I can. So far it has mostly been about helping them find the perfect seat for a concert with classical music or opera. A seat where you can see the hands of the pianist while keeping a distance to the brass section. Now I want to help them navigate through a website instead, but also to help the company that supplies the website. Help the user and the owner connect with me as an intermediary. Also, I want to do something that challenges me more.

Which leads me to my Javascript journey: I like the ability to do both frontend and backend in the same language, plus the user friendly syntax and availability that Javascript offers. I chose Chas for the good reputation, the friendly faces in the broschure and for walking distance.
